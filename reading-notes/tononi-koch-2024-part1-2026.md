## What We Know (from accessible sources)

### IIT 4.0 Core Concepts (Albantakis et al. 2023)

**Five Axioms** (properties of consciousness):
1. **Intrinsicality** — Consciousness exists for itself
2. **Information** — Consciousness is specific (differentiates states)
3. **Integration** — Consciousness is unified (irreducible)
4. **Exclusion** — Only maximally irreducible substrate is conscious
5. **Composition** — Consciousness has structure (multiple distinctions)

**Key metric**: Φ (integrated information)
- Φ > 0: conscious
- Φ ≈ 0: unconscious (feedforward networks, LLMs)
