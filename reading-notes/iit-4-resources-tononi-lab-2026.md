## Key Concepts (from publication list)

1. **Intrinsic existence** — Consciousness exists for itself (not for observers)
2. **Cause-effect power** — System must exert causal influence on itself
3. **Irreducibility** — Consciousness = integrated (cannot be decomposed)
4. **Exclusion** — Only maximally irreducible substrate is conscious
5. **Φ-structure** — Specific experience = specific cause-effect structure
