## The Φ Indicator

IIT proposes **Φ (phi)** as a measure of integrated information:

- **High Φ**: System has irreducible cause-effect structure → consciousness
- **Φ = 0**: System is reducible to independent parts → no consciousness
- **Feedforward networks** (like LLMs): Φ = 0 by architectural definition

**Implication for AI safety**: Adding recurrent connections, memory, planning to LLMs increases Φ — but this doesn't make AI "more conscious," it **changes the ontological category**.

This is more interesting than "AI has no consciousness." It means:
- Current LLMs: Φ ≈ 0 (feedforward)
- Agentic AI with feedback loops: Φ > 0 (some integration)
- **We are actively changing AI's ontological status by adding architecture**
